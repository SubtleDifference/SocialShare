Social Share - Example

Floating Example

This sits on the left hand side of your page or on the footer for smaller browsers in a fixed position. There is a 2 second delay before it slides into place

Inline Example

Place anywhere you want to have the share icons on your page.

Inline Example with Custom Theme

Just adding the class customtheme to your nav allows you to overide the overall look.

Generate your own HTML

A custom generator for the HTML output is available here https://subtledifference.com.au/social-share-generator, the HTML for the icons was generated from that form.

Raw files can be found here https://github.com/SubtleDifference/SocialShare

Don't forget to include the relevant ICON style for your theme
